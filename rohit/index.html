About TCS

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is a global leader in IT services, consulting, and business solutions. We partner with our clients to help them innovate and transform their businesses through the power of technology.

With a rich history spanning over five decades, TCS was established in 1968 and has since grown into one of the largest IT services companies in the world. Headquartered in Mumbai, India, TCS operates in over 46 countries and serves clients across diverse industries.

Our mission is to deliver excellence in every engagement, leveraging our deep industry expertise, innovative solutions, and global delivery capabilities. At TCS, we are committed to driving positive change and making a lasting impact on society and the environment.
